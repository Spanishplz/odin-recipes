Vegetarian Chili

return

Description

This spicy vegetarian chili is ready in no time and packed with vegetables, beans, and flavor! Break out your soup pot and fix up a batch of this delicious chili — it tastes even better the next day.

Because being vegetarian doesn’t mean you need to be leftout from these delicious recipes.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ medium onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 green bell peppers, chopped
  • 2 jalapeno pepers, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 (4 ounce) cans chopped green chile peppers, drained
  • 2 (12 ounce) package vegetarian burger crumbles
  • 3 (28 ounce) cans hole peeled tomatoes, crushed
  • ¼ chilli powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
  • 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
  • 1 (15 ounce) can black beans
  • 1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Stir in onion and season with oregano, salt, cumin, and bay leaves.
  2. Cook and stir until onion is tenredr; stir in celery, green bell peppers, jalapeños, and garlic.
  3. Add green chili peppers and cook until heated through.
  4. Stir in vegetarian burger crumbles; reduce heat to low, cover pot and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in tomatoes; season with chili powder and black pepper. Stir in kidney beans, garbanzo beans, and black beans.
  6. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 45 minutes.
  7. Stir in the corn and continue cooking for 5 minutes before serving.
